My Fiery Origin

Inferno Complaints began as a personal project after years of watching legitimate complaints disappear into bureaucratic black holes. As a solo developer with government experience, I built this platform to ensure no grievance would ever be ignored again.

"I may work alone, but I burn twice as hot. Every complaint that comes through this system gets my personal attention until it's resolved."

This isn't some faceless corporation - it's one determined individual using technology to hold power accountable. No committees, no bureaucracy, just relentless focus on results.
