Road Maintenance

How a Massive Pothole Was Fixed After 6 Months of Neglect

Ramesh Patel
June 15, 2024

For six long months, residents of Greenfield Colony endured a dangerous pothole that had developed on the main road. What started as a small depression grew into a 3-foot-wide crater that damaged vehicles and caused several accidents...

The municipal corporation repeatedly ignored complaints until our platform amplified the issue with photographic evidence and traffic accident data...

Official Response (Public Works Department)

"We acknowledge the delay in addressing this issue. After reviewing the complaint and supporting evidence, we prioritized this repair. The pothole has now been permanently fixed with high-quality materials, and we've implemented a new monitoring system for road conditions in this area."

Water Supply

10 Days Without Water: How a Neighborhood Fought Back

Priya Menon
June 10, 2024

When the taps ran dry in Sector 7, residents initially thought it was a temporary outage. But as days turned into a week with no explanation from authorities, frustration grew. Children couldn't bathe, restaurants closed, and hospitals struggled...

Our investigation revealed a bureaucratic standoff between two departments, each blaming the other for the crisis...

Official Response (Water Department)

"We regret the inconvenience caused to residents. The issue stemmed from a valve failure compounded by inter-departmental communication gaps. We've now repaired the valve, established a 24/7 emergency response team, and created a joint task force to prevent similar situations."

Electricity

Dark Streets, Bright Results: Fixing Faulty Street Lights

Amit Sharma
June 5, 2024

The children's park near Gandhi Nagar became a danger zone after dark when all 12 street lights stopped working. Parents stopped bringing children, and the area saw a spike in petty crimes. Despite individual complaints to the electricity board, no action was taken for three weeks...

Our platform documented the safety hazards with nighttime videos and crime statistics, forcing authorities to act...

Official Response (Electricity Board)

"We apologize for the delay in addressing this issue. All street lights have now been repaired, and we've replaced the outdated control system that caused the failure. Additionally, we're implementing a new public lighting maintenance schedule with priority given to parks and residential areas."
