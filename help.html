Getting Started

Welcome to Inferno Complaints, your platform for holding authorities accountable. Here's how to begin:

  • Create an account (optional but recommended for tracking)
  • Browse existing complaints to see how the process works
  • Prepare your evidence before filing a new complaint
  • Understand the complaint categories to file properly

Our system is designed to amplify citizen voices through documented, evidence-backed complaints.

Filing Complaints

To file an effective complaint that gets results:

  • Be specific about dates, locations, and officials involved
  • Include clear evidence (photos, documents, videos)
  • Focus on one issue per complaint for better tracking
  • Provide contact information for follow-up questions
  • Check for existing complaints about the same issue

Well-documented complaints are processed faster and get better responses from authorities.

Submitting Proof

Evidence strengthens your complaint. Follow these guidelines:

  • Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, PDF, MP4, DOC, XLS (25MB max total)
  • Photo evidence: Clear, timestamped, with location if possible
  • Documents: Redact personal information before uploading
  • Videos: Short clips (under 2 minutes) with clear context
  • Witness statements: Include contact information

You can add more evidence to existing complaints using your complaint ID.

Tracking Complaints

Monitor your complaint's progress through these methods:

  • Check your email for status updates
  • Use your complaint ID to track online
  • View our blog for resolved complaint stories
  • Response times vary by department (typically 7-14 days)

If your complaint hasn't received a response in 30 days, contact us for follow-up.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

Most complaints receive an initial response within 7-14 business days. Complex cases may take longer. We follow up with authorities weekly until we receive a response.

Can I file anonymous complaints?

While you can submit without creating an account, valid contact information is required for officials to respond. We never share your details without permission.

What types of complaints are not accepted?

We don't handle personal disputes, private business complaints, or matters before courts. Illegal content or harassment reports will be removed.

How do I escalate an unresolved complaint?

After 30 days with no response, email us at complaints@inferno.com with your complaint ID. We'll initiate official follow-up procedures.

Can I edit my complaint after submission?

While you can't edit the original complaint, you can submit additional information or corrections using your complaint ID.
